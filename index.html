Founded in June 2017

Visit H1gherMusicOfficial.com

Some facts about the company:

  • Are a global hip-hop label
  • Have an international network formed mainly in Seattle and Seoul
  • 11 artists from Seoul, Korea
  • 5 artists from U.S.
  • Concerts while on global tours
  • Each artist has total freedom with their music
  • Artists are encouraged to be creative with their work
  • The contract for an artist usually is for 3 months
